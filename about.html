Hi, I'm Sophia Navarro! An aspiring animator and illustrator based in New York State.

I currently attend Mercy University at Dobbs Ferry where I hope to receive a Bachelor’s degree in Fine Arts by 2028.

Growing up in New York all my life, I remember roaming the busy streets of Manhattan with my family and seeing all the different types of artists vending their work. From handcrafted jewelry to spray-painted space-themed canvases, I grew to love the art of putting pen on paper. As a result, I’ve devoted much of my life to all things related to the visual arts like animation.

Right now my plan is to work on my portfolio and get my foot in the door!
